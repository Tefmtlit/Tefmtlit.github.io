Joining a Booked Meeting

Use the meeting card on the room controller or join by Meeting ID.

üîΩ

Meetings scheduled using the room calendar appear as Meeting Cards (A).

  1. Find your meeting in Meeting Cards.
  2. Tap the meeting card to expand it (1).
  3. Select Join (2) to enter the meeting.
Tip: If the meeting was booked correctly, this avoids re‚Äëentering details like Meeting ID and passcode.
Room controller showing Meeting Cards.

Manually join an existing meeting

If no meeting card is visible, join manually:

  1. Press Join (3) for Zoom or Microsoft Teams (4) for Teams.
  2. Select Zoom or Microsoft Teams as appropriate.
  3. Enter the Meeting ID, then press Join.
  4. When prompted, enter the passcode.
Note: The meeting organizer may need to manually admit the room.

Creating a New Meeting

Start an impromptu Zoom meeting and invite attendees.

üîΩ

Start a new (impromptu) meeting

  1. Press New Meeting (1).
  2. A Zoom meeting starts immediately.

Invite attendees

  1. Press Invite.
  2. Choose:
  • Internal contacts: search & select colleagues.
  • Email tab: invite external participants.
Tip: Tap the meeting title at the top of the screen to view the Meeting ID & passcode.

Direct Calling (Internal Zoom contacts only)

Call one or more internal Zoom contacts from the Contacts tab.

üîΩ

Call a single person

  1. Open the Contacts tab.
  2. Search for the person you want to call.
  3. Press Meet to start the call.

Call multiple people

  1. Press Select to enable multi‚Äëselect.
  2. Choose all desired contacts.
  3. Press Start to begin the call.
