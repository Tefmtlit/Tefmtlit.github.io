Joining a Booked Meeting

Use the meeting card from the room calendar, or join manually using a Meeting ID.

‚åÑ

Join a scheduled meeting (recommended)

Meetings scheduled using the room calendar appear as Meeting Cards (A).

  1. Locate your meeting in the Meeting Cards area.
  2. Tap the meeting card to expand it. (1)
  3. Select Join (2) to enter the meeting.
Tip: If the meeting was booked correctly, this method avoids re-entering details like Meeting ID and passcode.
Room Controller with Meeting Cards.

Manually join an existing meeting

If the meeting card is not visible, join manually using the meeting details.

  1. Press Join (3).
  2. Select Zoom or Microsoft Teams.
  3. Enter the Meeting ID.
  4. When prompted, enter the passcode.
Note: In some meetings, the organizer may need to manually admit the room into the meeting.

Direct Calling (Internal Zoom contacts only)

Call one or more internal Zoom contacts directly from the Contacts tab.

‚åÑ

Call a single person

  1. Select the Contacts tab.
  2. Search for the person you want to call.
  3. Press Meet to start the call.

Call multiple people

  1. Press Select to enable multi-select.
  2. Choose all desired contacts.
  3. Press Start to begin the call.
Reminder: Direct calling works for internal Zoom contacts only.

Screen Sharing

Share content using the room controller and your laptop (Zoom app or share.zoom.us).

‚åÑ

Step 1: Start sharing from the room controller

  1. Press Share Content on the room controller.
  2. Note the Sharing Key displayed on the room screen.(A)

Step 2: Share from your computer

Option A ‚Äî Zoom desktop app

  1. Open the Zoom app on your computer.
  2. Click Share Screen.
  3. Enter the Sharing Key from the room display.(A)

Option B ‚Äî Web browser

  1. Open a browser and go to share.zoom.us
  2. Enter the Meeting ID and passcode shown on the room display (A).
