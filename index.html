Joining a Booked Meeting

Use the meeting card on the room controller or join manually.

üîΩ

Meetings scheduled using the room calendar appear as Meeting Cards (A).

  1. Find your meeting in Meeting Cards.
  2. Tap the meeting card to expand it (1).
  3. Select Join (2) to enter the meeting.
Tip: If the meeting was booked correctly, this avoids typing Meeting ID and passcode.
Room controller showing Meeting Cards.

Manually join an existing meeting

If no meeting card appears:

  1. Press Join (3) for Zoom or Teams (4).
  2. Select either Zoom or Microsoft Teams.
  3. Enter the Meeting ID.
  4. When prompted, enter the passcode.
Note: The meeting organizer may need to admit the room.

Direct Calling

Call internal Zoom contacts from the controller.

üîΩ

Call a single person

Reminder: Works only with internal Zoom contacts.
  1. Open Contacts (1).
  2. Search (2) for a person.
  3. Press Meet (3).
Searching & starting a direct call.

Call multiple people

  1. Press Select (1).
  2. Choose participants.
  3. Press Start (2).
